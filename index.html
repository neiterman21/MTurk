Cheat game (Bullshit) Rules:

  • You must have a working microphone and speakers.
  • Please talk directly to the microphone
  • The goal is to get rid of all the cards.
  • Each turn you can drop up to 4 cards.
  • Your claim must be 1 above or 1 below the last claim.
  • You will need to lie well to win
  • Play fair within the rules of the game. if you will falsely report you don't hear the other side you will not get paid.

The cards:

There are 52 cards each card with a digit (J = 11 , Q = 12, K = 13 , A = 14/1)

The beginning:

  • Each player is dealt 8 cards
  • You play by turn
  • First player is randomly chosen.  

The goal:

The goal is to get rid of the cards. first player to rid of all it's cards WIN!

Turns:

Each turn the player can

  • Take a card
  • Drop cards and make a claim
  • Call a cheat

Dropping cards:

You may drop 1-4 card each turn. You can claim to drop cards 1 higher or 1 lower than the last claim. 

example: last claim is Q. you may claim to drop Ks or Js.

In order to prevent people from not using the microphone or stating illegal claims. Each claim must be verified by your opponent.  

If you feel your opponent is misusing the claim verification, that is, your claims are within the rules but your opponents reported you, you may report him back and we will manually check who is correct.

 

Calling a cheat:

 You may call a cheat on your opponent's claim, that is, stating that the cards put down are different than the opponent's claim. If you are correct your opponent will take all the card on the board. If you are wrong, you will take the cards.
